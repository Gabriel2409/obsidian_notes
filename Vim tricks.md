#vim

- corrections from insert mode: 
    - `<c-h>` to delete char
    - `<c-w>` to delete word
    - `<c-u>` to delete whole line
- paste  from insert mode: `<c-r>0` (0 is yank register)

- reselect last visual selection: `gv`
- Go to the other end of visual selection: `o`
